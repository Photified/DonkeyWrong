How To Play

**Mobile:** Tap left side to move left, right side to move right. Tap center to jump.

**Desktop:** Arrow Keys to move, Spacebar to jump.

**Mission:** You start at the TOP. DK is at the BOTTOM throwing ANTI-GRAVITY barrels! Climb DOWN to the green exit door at the bottom right.
